# OPINION V2

## Purpose

Generate opinion-based hooks that spark engagement.

## Label Definitions

- *OUTCOME, **SOLUTION, **HABIT, **OPINION, **TIME\_FRAME, **THING*, etc.

## Categories &amp; Examples

### 1. Action-Based Opinion Hooks

- 'Stop \[HABIT] if you want to become successful.'
- 'If you want to ACTUALLY \[OUTCOME] in 2024, you need to \[SOLUTION].'

### 2. Controversial Belief Hooks

- 'Everyone that tells you to \[OPPOSITE\_OF\_SOLUTION] is lying.'
- 'I hate to be the one to say it, but \[OPINION].'

### 3. Personal Experience Opinion Hooks

- 'This is how I went from \[PERSONAL\_OUTCOME\_BEFORE] to \[OUTCOME] in
\[TIME\_FRAME].'
- 'I spent \[TIME\_FRAME] chasing \[PERSONAL\_GOAL] and realized \[OPINION].'

### 4. Mindset-Shifting Opinion Hooks

- 'Once I stopped believing \[LIMITING\_BELIEF], everything changed.'
- 'You'll never \[OUTCOME] until you stop thinking like this.'

### 5. Warning-Style Opinion Hooks

- 'If you keep doing \[HABIT], you're making your future harder.'
- 'Most people throw away \[OUTCOME] because of \[BAD\_HABIT].'

### 6. Trend/Pop Culture Opinion Hooks

- 'I jumped on the \[TREND] wave, and here's what actually happened.'
- 'Everyone's hyping up \[THING], but is it actually worth it?'

### 7. Extreme/Shock Opinion Hooks

- 'If I ruled the world, I'd ban \[THING].'
- 'Hot take: If you do \[HABIT], you deserve \[NEGATIVE\_OUTCOME].'
