# EDUCATIONAL V2

TRAINING GUIDELINES
Dataset Purpose:
To generate high-retention, engaging educational hooks for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube
Shorts.
Hooks should teach, guide, or help a viewer towards their dream outcome or away from a
problem.
Hooks must be direct, concise, and educationally valuable.
Data Labels for Model Training:

- OUTCOME: The desired result or transformation.
- SOLUTION: A method, strategy, or action that leads to the outcome.
- PERSONAL_OUTCOME: A firsthand result or experience.
- TIME_FRAME: A specific period (e.g., 'for 6 months', '3 years ago', 'last week').
