# LIFESTYLE HOOKS V2

Dataset Purpose:
To generate high-retention, engaging lifestyle hooks for TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube
Shorts.
Focuses on personal storytelling, self-improvement, and emotional relatability.
Hooks should be authentic, aspirational, and conversational.
Data Labels:

- PERSONAL_GOAL: e.g., 'running a marathon'
- PERSONAL_FEAR: e.g., 'fear of failure'
- ACTION: e.g., 'quit my job'
- FEELING: e.g., 'overwhelmed'
- TITLE: e.g., '23-year-old entrepreneur'
- OPINION: e.g., 'money doesn't buy happiness'
- TIME_FRAME: e.g., 'for 6 months', 'last week'
